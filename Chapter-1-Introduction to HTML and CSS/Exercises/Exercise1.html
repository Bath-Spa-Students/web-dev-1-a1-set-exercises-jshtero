Ingredients to make Spicy Beef Caldereta:

Just a quick description of the dish:

Caldereta is a stew made with beef, similar to curry it is a filipino dish that is influenced by the Spanish. The dish caldereta can be compared to the Filipino dish known as Menudo. The word caldera means cauldron in spanish.

  • 1/4 Cup Canola Oil
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 1 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • 2 pounds bottom round roast, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups water
  • 10 Thai chili peppers, minced
  • 1/2 cup green olives, pitted
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 small green bell pepper, seeded, cored and cut into cubes
  • 1 small red bell pepper, seeded, cored and cut into cubes
  • salt and pepper to taste
