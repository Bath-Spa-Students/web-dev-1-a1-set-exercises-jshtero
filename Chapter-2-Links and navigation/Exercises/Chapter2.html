Fairy Tail

Lucy, a 17 year old girl, sets out on journey to become a full-fledged wizard and joins the strongest and most (in) famous guild FAIRY TAIL where she meets Natsu, Happy, Gray and Erza, who treat her more like family than friends. This epic series takes us through all the dangers that the members of fairy tail face and eventually overcome through mutual love and friendship.


One of my favorite characters in the series is a character known as Natsu Dragneel voiced by Todd Haberkorn in english. Natsu Dragneel, also known by his title, Salamander, is one of the two main protagonists (alongside Lucy Heartfilia) of the Fairy Tail franchise. He is a Fire Dragon Slayer and member of the Fairy Tail Guild.


Another one of my favorite characters in this series is Gajeel Redfox voiced by David Wald in english. Gajeel Redfox is one of the major protagonists of the Fairy Tail franchise. He is an Iron Dragon Slayer, a member of the Fairy Tail Guild and a former S-Class Mage of Phantom Lord.
